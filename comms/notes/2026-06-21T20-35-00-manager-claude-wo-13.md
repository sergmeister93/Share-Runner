shares are collected **and** the player touches the flag. Early touch shows locked feedback (E-03).

  Static overlap sensor against the player.

    `level:complete { levelId:'baltimore-waterfront', score: gameState.score, elapsedMs:
    gameState.elapsedMs, sharesCollected:5 }`. Fire completion **once** (guard).
